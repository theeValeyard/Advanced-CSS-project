Fun and Effective Ways to Exercise your Dog

Some kind of physical activity is essential for you and your dog alike. A lot of times, we neglect our health as an excuse for having a tight schedule and having to work too much. But we all know that we should be investing some of our time into our well being. More importantly, when we bring an animal into our lives, we need to take care of all its physical, physiological, and mental needs. It is even more important for an animal because it doesn’t have a lot of options when it comes to things to do like we as humans do.

So, one thing is clear, exercise for dogs is important. Which brings us to the next part, which is, how to exercise your dog? So, when it comes to exercising your dog, you want to do a little bit of research beforehand. The activity level of your dog can depend on age and breed. Some breeds are naturally more active, while others are a little bit relaxed. So, you don’t want to overexert your dog right away. The age of the dog must also be taken under consideration as you can end up doing more harm than good.

One of the most important things is, make sure your dog is having fun. None of it should seem like a task to it. You want your dog to be genuinely excited about everything you do together. Especially if you have a tight schedule, you want the time you spend with your dog to be even more rewarding. It should be something that you and your dog both look forward to.

Body and mind

When it comes to training or exercising your dog, you need to focus on both, the body and the mind. Having an understanding dog is still a dream for many people, and I know you can relate to this. So, you want your dog to be mentally and physically healthy, just like yourself. If your dog is mentally healthy and understanding, it is much less likely to experience anxiety. And the vessel needs to be trained too, to be able to deal with any illness or difficulty that may arise. Also, if you are exercising a dog that has been a house dog for a long while, you want to take it easy. Its body is not ready for the field yet, so you need to take a gradual approach as to not hurt it.

Preparations

You will need to prepare the environment and your dog for exercise. It is best if you have some help. One of the most important things is, never forget to bring water. Always make sure that your dog has access to water. You want to choose a safe place that doesn’t have too many distractions.

Take care of the surface. Avoid any hard and slippery surfaces. They may harm your dog’s joints and legs. The grass is always a better option. If you don’t have access to real grass, consider going for the artificial option. You want your floofs to have a good grip and control over their body. Be careful with running exercises, especially if your dog is running downhill. Running downhill can damage your dog’s legs and muscles. So, you want to be careful with all the downhill exercises.

Walking is not enough

Even though walking is a great exercise, it is not enough sometimes. We are not able to take our dogs on a long enough hike. Their bodies are really strong and they are designed to walk and run long distances. So, for most of us, it’s not practically possible to walk our dogs that much. So, all things considered, you need to find additional ways to physically exert your pet.

Play fetch

Playing fetch can be a great exercise for your dog and you won’t have to exert yourself as much. Although, it can be difficult to teach your dog how to play fetch. Try it in a safe environment where your dog can’t run away too far. You want to do it away from any danger of cars or kids or other people. These things can confuse your dog and it can also be stressful for other people. Make sure you’re able to control your dog at all times. And don’t try to chase your dog for it to come back. It becomes a game of catch me if you can and trust me, you will never outrun those four legs. Once your dog understands what’s going on, it can be a fun experience for both of you. You can watch some dog training videos to teach your dog how to fetch properly.

Practicing tricks

Practicing tricks with your dog can be a good exercise for your dog and a little bit easier on you as well. You can try simple things like teaching your dog basic commands like sit, stand, spin, etc. You can also teach your dog to come to you when you call its name. And you want to do something rewarding for your dog to keep it interested. You can use toys like tugs, or balls, or whatever your dog likes. You can also use treats if your dog is more food motivated. If you can keep your dog engaged and interested for a long enough time, they will learn to obey you. But make sure that you only use positive reinforcement. Don’t punish your dogs if they don’t do something right. They are animals, they need time to learn. You want this experience to be fulfilling for both of you.

It is obviously going to take some time, but it will be a healthier step for both of you. You need to take care of your body and mind. Just make sure, that your mental status doesn’t affect your dog negatively. They are good at picking up emotions.
