How I got Sucked into an Ocean of Manga Comics

Mangas comics are graphic novels from Japan and one of the best things you can stumble upon. The illustrations, the story, the way it is told, everything about mangas is just mind blowing. There are mangas out there that will leave you rolling on the floor laughing and at the same time tell a heart wrenching story to leave you in tears. One thing you need to keep in mind is, that mangas are read from right to left and not the left to right like in the west. You start reading from the top right panel and then read on left. Here is how you can dip your toes in the ocean of manga comics with our top picks.

One Piece

One piece is a Shōnen Manga series by a wonderful man called Echiiro Oda. It is considered one of the best mangas of all time and you will find yourself instantly addicted to it. It’s about a young guy named Luffy who sails the oceans, looking for the world’s greatest treasure called ‘One Piece’ with his friends in the pursuit of being the pirate king. It has some of the best storytelling and a mind blowing storyline with one of the best world settings of all time. There is a reason it is considered to be the number one manga. You will instantly fall in love with the characters and their journey (character development). But the only way to find out for sure is to start reading today. Trust me, you won’t regret it. It is going to be an epic adventure where you’re going to laugh your ears off and cry like a toddler, but it will all be worth it. So, go check it out now.

Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is a historical manga by Makoto Yukimura. It is set in the Dane controlled England in the 11th century and features the Vikings in a really picturesque way. The story is about a teenage warrior called Thorfinn. It is about his journey as he advances towards being strong enough to kill his commander by challenging him to a duel. Thorfinn tries to avenge his father’s death. Vinland saga has some mind blowing storyline with some really strong characters. If you like Vikings, there is no way you should be missing out on this gem.

Berserk

Berserk is a manga series by Kentaro Miura. It is set in a dark fantasy world inspired by medieval Europe. Berserk is an old manga, but it is considered one of the finest. It has one of the saddest stories that are good enough to make a grown man cry. It has a fine storyline with some fine artwork. The story is about a young mercenary called guts who has a really sad story and the hardships of his life have turned him into a savage beast. It is definitely one of the best manga series of all time. However, it may not be the best option to recommend it to a younger audience. Still, this masterpiece is worth checking out for sure.

The Drop of God

The drop of god is a Japanese manga series about wine. So, if you’re a wine drinker or admirer, this will be a good fit for you. It was written and created by Tadashi Agi. It is about a guy who travels the world with his father as a child learning about wine from a young age. The story starts as our protagonist comes to know of his father’s demise and in order to inherit his father’s legacy he must identify and describe the 13 wines the way his late father would. The first 12 are known as ‘Twelve Apostles’ and the thirteenth one is called the drop of god. He has a really great sense of smell and he competes with his adopted brother who is also a wine critique for his father’s will. And all the wines mentioned in the series are authentic. So, if wines interest you, this should too.

The Promised Neverland

The promised neverland is a manga series written by Kaiu Shirai and illustrated by Posuka Demizu. The story is about an 11 year old orphan girl who lives in an orphanage home in the year 2045 with other young children. She is a bright eyed and happy child who always aces in her exams and lives happily. The only rule of the orphanage is to not go out of the gate. They receive really great living conditions and lots of love from their caretaker who they call Mama. Life is great. But one day, one of the children named Conny is adopted and taken into the outside world. Emma and her friends follow him to return his toy, to find out that he is butchered at the gate and that all the children are raised humanely to eventually be butchered and sold as meat to a higher species called the demons. And Emma and her friends begin to look for an escape.

We hope we were able to serve you in some way and we hope that you had a good time reading it. All of these series are masterpieces and you should really check them out. Be safe and have a great day, we’ll see you next time.
