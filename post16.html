2019 Shows you Shouldn't Miss

2019 gave us some really great shows. And there is a good reason why you shouldn't be missing out on them. For starters, they are easy to follow and easier to get into. And they all share some of the very unique and thought provoking ideas in the form of good humor and a better storyline.

Here is a list of some of the best shows from the year 2019. And you should definitely give them a try. Keep reading to know which of them are for you. Also, there are no spoilers, so you don't need to worry about ruining your TV series if you haven't yet seen any.

Fleabag

Fleabag is a comedy show about a young woman who is trying to cope up with hassles of everyday life in the city of London. It is a show about a dry witted angry woman who tries to deal with tragedy by rejecting everyone who tries to help her. The show is really great, it revolves around a single character known as fleabag. Fleabag is one of the best written characters of all time. The depth of her character needs to be understood through different dimensions rather than one. We see the entire show from the perspective of the main character. She reveals angles to her personality that we had not expected. The show uses perspective to structure the entire story.

It is not a very big series, you can ideally complete it one sitting. It has two seasons with six episodes each, and each episode is approximately 25 minutes long. Season two of fleabag aired in March 2019. It is an award winning mini series and when it comes to being entertained, just know that all those awards were well earned.

Barry

Barry is a strange show but in a good way. It is about a guy named Barry who is a professional assassin who accidentally finds out that his real passion is acting. So, he joins an acting class to follow his dreams. He is a very skilled killer, but he is a terrible actor. The show is as brutal as it is funny. He wants to be good at acting because of the fact that he really likes it, and that all that killing has made me lose a part of himself. It is definitely a show worth watching, it is a perfect projection of comedy and action.

The show has two seasons with eight episodes each and the main character Barry is played by Bill Hader. It is an engaging series with a unique storyline. So, there is a good chance that you would not regret watching Barry. It is an award winning show and you will soon find out why, if you actually choose to watch it.

Succession

Succession is one of the most hilarious shows you will ever watch. It is a business comedy. So, if you are a business person, you would really like this show. The basic theme of the show is that the Logan family is widely known to control the biggest media and entertainment giant in the world. The story revolves around, who would inherit the company after the father retires. The father feels that he is growing old and that he would have to retire soon. So, he tells his three sons and daughter to prove themselves. The sons are absolutely terrible and stupid and none of them are capable of running the company. The daughter seems most capable of actually being able to handle the company. But she is the youngest of her siblings so proving herself to her father also becomes challenging.

However, watching them try to win their father over is entertaining and amusing. It is another award winning show with two seasons with ten episodes each. Succession is a brilliant show and it is definitely worth your time.

Chernobyl

Chernobyl is a mini series with just five episodes in total. The theme is based around one of the worst manmade disasters ever faced by humanity. Which was, the Chernobyl nuclear disaster of 1986. The show is considered one of the best TV shows of not just the year, but the entire decade. It tells the story of a real incident and it will send a shiver down your spine. The show is about how enormous the damage really was, and how it influenced mankind everywhere. People who lived to tell about never really recovered. The pilot episode of the show is one of the most captivating episodes of all time.

The show is pretty heavy though, and for good reason. It can get really rough at times, to even imagine what those people actually went through. For some audience, it may be too much to even watch. But it was important from the show's perspective to illustrate the real image of the severity of the problem. But the show is really great, it has great actors, great background score, great storytelling. Everything about the show is mind blowing.

Chernobyl mini series is the perfect mix of entertainment, information, justice, and tribute. It is one of those shows that you should absolutely watch and it will change the way you look at media content. You would have a new found respect for the TV industry.

Killing Eve

Killing Eve is a show about two very intelligent women who are trying to catch each other, one being a professional spy and the other being a psycho killer. Two seasons of the show have been released with eight episodes each, so, sixteen in total. It is not a very big show, you can binge watch it on a weekend. It is a great show with a tinge of comedy and a great storyline. Both of these women are equally obsessed with each other and their characters are so well written, that you would not know which one to root for. You probably would not have seen a cat and mouse game quite like this one. The cast of the show is really fine, all of the actors are really skilled and amazing.
