Who Am I?

I don't know myself as well as I'd like to. So, I don't exactly know who I am. But I'll try to tell you as best as I can. I'm just a random 20 year old, and I look like I'm 16, because I look like a scared child and I'm 5'2. So, I'm not quite sure how I mustered up the courage to come to a land that's the furthest away from my home. I guess, that makes me brave, or maybe that makes me stupid. But I'm smart enough to know I'm a little bit of both.

I like to write, I like to paint, I like to read, I like to code, and I like to make bookmarks out of scrap paper a day before the deadline, because my brain won't let me get started otherwise. I like to pretend to be tougher than I actually am. So, I guess, in a way, I'm just like you.

I'm a smalltowm girl who left home to see the world, and I guess I'm starting with a small Canadian Town. I'm from the land of Castles and Stories. I'm from the desert of Rajasthan. And I like to walk, but I have a terrible sense of direction, literal or metaphorical. I get distracted easily. I forget things, I like to watch the sun go down on the ocean. I'm just human, I guess, trying to blend in and run away at the same time.

There's a very good chance that you will be more confused after reading this than before, because I don't have the words today. They come to me when they want to. I guess today I'll have to go look for them.
