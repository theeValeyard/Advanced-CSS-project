Top 10 Piano Melodies That Make You Feel Good

Music is the language of the soul. Music is the escape that lets you have a moment of silence in the midst of all the chaos. So, here is a list of piano melodies that make you feel good, enchanted and lost in your own passion when you need it the most. History is no short of genius composers who left the world with their legacies that live to this very day. Art lives on, and it makes the maker immortal. So, let us take a moment to appreciate these beautiful compositions. Trust me, these will make your day.

Moonlight Sonata - Beethoven

Beethoven was one of the best composers and pianists there has ever been. And Moonlight Sonata is one of the most impressive piano pieces of all time. It makes you feel calm, composed and relaxed. Movement third of the moonlight sonata is absolute perfection. If you ever feel like you are out of motivation or inspiration, this piece can help you get your spark back. It does get a little serious sometimes, but that just adds up to its charm. It is a wonderful composition and you don't want to miss out on it.

Mariage d'Amour - Paul de Senneville

Mariage d'Amour translates to a marriage of love, it is a piece of French solo piano music. It is one of the most peaceful piano pieces out there. But on the other hand, the passion and chaos that it carries within is unmatched. It makes you look back on your life with a feeling of longing and peace at the same time. It is a really inspiring, yet sad composition. And it makes you feel everything at once, and it is definitely worth checking out. Strange thing is, everyone, can relate to this piece. So, check it out right now, it is worth your time.

Rondo Alla Turca - Mozart

This is one of the most famous piano pieces ever composed in the history of music. And chances are you have already heard it. It has a really jolly and happy feel to it. It is definitely one of the piano melodies that make you feel good. Mozart was one of the greatest musicians who ever walked this earth. And if you do a little research, you will know exactly what I mean. Rondo Alla Turca is also known as Piano Sonata No. 11 by Mozart.

This will give you a Deja Vu, and you will instantly recognize it. The reason being, it is ridiculously famous. So, check it out, it is definitely something worth looking into.

Beethoven's Silence - Ernesto Cortázar II

Beethoven's silence is a little serious, but it still is a gorgeous composition. It feels like the chill of a winter's night, but at the same time, it feels like the stars that follow. Ernesto was a Mexican composer who faced a lot of tragedy in his life early on, and Beethoven's Silence conveys the story of his life in perfect context. It is sad and compassionate at the same time. And you should look into it, you won't be disappointed.

River Flows In You - Yiruma

Yiruma is a really great South Korean - British pianist and 'river flows in you' is one of his best compositions. It has that sweet feel good factor combined with great delicacy and skill. It is a romantic piece and you can listen to it anytime. River flows in you pleases the mind and body alike. It will leave you feeling fascinated, wonderful and beautiful. It helps you appreciate life when it is actually happening to you, signifying a really important lesson that you should never forget, that you should never wait for life to happen to you. Life is already happening, you just need to be brave enough to be a part of it.

Fur Elise - Beethoven

Beethoven is one of the very few musical legends who were so dedicated to their craft that they did not mind dying for it. Fur Elise is one of the most eminent music pieces of all time, so, there is a good chance that you have already heard it. It gives you that serious chilly feeling, along with great passion infused with a skill that matches no other. The piece was released 40 years after Beethoven died. Some people say that it was Beethoven's old flame. Either way, it is one of Beethoven's most popular songs. You really need to see this.

Time Forgets - Yiruma

Yiruma is one of the best modern day pianists, so people find it easier to relate to him. He is a really skilled musician and his music makes you feel at peace with everything and yourself. His piano melodies can definitely be considered among piano melodies that make you feel good. Time forgets is a sweet, calm and peaceful composition that you can meditate with, walk with, or do any kind of creative work with. It brings out the creative genius in you, and it makes you feel calm. If you are a fan of piano melodies, his music will make you feel connected in a really good way.

Canon in D - Johann Pachelbel

Canon in D is one of the most eminent pieces of the piano. And it is, no doubt, a wonderful composition. Its name is Canon but a lot of people refer to it as Canon in D as it is fairly popular in D key. Johann Pachelbel was a German composer and Canon is the most popular piece he ever composed. It is soothing to the soul and it makes you feel you feel at ease. If you are ever having a bad day, there is a good chance that it will brighten your mood.

Gymnopédie No. 1 - Erik Satie

Erik Satie was a very skilled French pianist and his compositions are absolutely wonderful. Gymnopédie is a great piece and due to its highly cinematic composition, it has been used in numerous films. It is a serious, painful, heartfelt and demonically beautiful piece. It is one of the best pieces of music ever written or performed. The flow of emotions is just, mesmerizing. You should really check this out. It is heartbreakingly beautiful.

Nuvole Bianche - Ludovico Einaudi

Nuvole Bianche means white clouds, and it perfectly summarizes the piece, for its peaceful and comfortable nature. Now, this one is one of the piano melodies that make you feel good. It makes you feel hopeful and strong through the tough times and makes you believe that life really can be a beautiful journey. It symbolizes hope, like the light at the end of the tunnel. Even if it is far, it makes you feel like you are getting somewhere and you are working towards something that would make you really happy at the end of the day.

The light and comforting feeling of this piece is unmatched. And it is worth mentioning again, that it is heartbreakingly beautiful. Nuvole Bianche is no doubt, a great composer. And if you can spare some time, you should look into his other stuff as well.

In Conclusion

Music can make everything better, and the piano is a grand instrument. These melodies can make your heart weep and soul smile at the same time. These piano melodies make you feel good and inspired and bring out the creative genius in you. It can get you through a tough phase in life, and help shape a better perspective towards life and happiness.

If you are a musician or someone who really likes music, this is something that you absolutely need to check out, if you haven't already. This is some of the best music ever created in history. These musical geniuses lived absolutely fascinating lives and their music conveys what words never could. So, if you admire the art of music, this article has been dedicated to you. Keep growing, keep inspiring, and keep living.
