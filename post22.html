How Music Makes you Feel Good

Music is the feel-good mechanism for almost everyone, and that's because it works. It can make every situation better. Music impacts us in such a powerful way that people tend to get addicted to it. The way music makes you feel can be life-saving in your darkest hour. Music has saved countless lives. And here are some scientifically proven ways how music can make you feel good in your everyday life.

Music reduces stress

Most of us struggle with stress, it's almost a part of our lives. And a good playlist can save the day every time. Some soothing and calming music can act as meditation for your tormented mind. So, next time you feel stressed out, put on some calming music and go for a walk. You'll come home with a smile on your face. The heaviness in your mind and heart will melt into the musical notes.

Makes you healthier and increases endurance

It is scientifically proven that listening to music releases dopamine, which is also known as the pleasure chemical. So, it automatically triggers your mind to release the feel-good hormone. Music has a positive impact on your body. It makes you healthier and increases your endurance to pain.

So, put on some of your favorite music and lose yourself in the symphonies. It was found in a lot of studies that music literally makes your metabolism stronger. So, listening to music is not a waste of time as many people will have you believe. It's actually great for your health.

Music helps with anxiety

Anxiety can be a serious problem and it can be really dangerous if it is taken lightly. So, you should not risk your health and safety by not taking professional help. It is a real thing, and its symptoms must never be ignored. But music can help a great deal with anxiety. Music relaxes the mind and makes you feel calmer and more in control, even if you think nothing is going right.

Listening to happy and uplifting music can save you from recurring bouts of anxiety. Music can save a life, and lift the burden off your shoulders. Happiness is a choice, and it's your job to choose what's best for you. Don't forget to serve yourself.

Make you smarter and improves memory

It's scientifically proven that people who play any kind of instrument have enhanced motor and reasoning skills. Research shows a visible correlation between learning to play an instrument as a child and being successful later in the future. But something as simple as just listening to music can also make you smarter and improve your memory.

ave you ever wondered why it was comparatively easier to remember song lyrics than anything else? That's because music sharpens your memory. So, if you are studying for a test, you can put on some music without lyrics in it, to memorize it better. The lyrics can be confusing while studying, so playing melody or calm music in the background can help you focus better. Later, whatever you memorized with the tune in your head is much easier to recall than something memorized without it. You can also use video game soundtracks for studying. That music is specifically designed to keep you focused.

Angry music improves performance

Some rock, metal or heavy metal can enhance your efficiency and performance. Fast or angry music helps you focus better and it helps you harness your aggression into something positive. It makes you feel like you're in control and it's easier to channel all your energy into something with the help of fast-paced music.

The music which is aggressive in nature can fill you up with energy and determination, even when you are not up for the task. Music can change your mood according to the need of the hour, and aggressive music and video game tracks can be really useful in our daily lives. So, it is something that you should definitely check out.

Music can give you a better workout session

Music and exercise make a perfect pair. People find it easier to workout with music than otherwise. That's because music keeps the mood and spirits up, while also making you feel good and strong. It increases your tolerance to pain and keeps you going for a longer period of time.

EDM or electronic dance music fits great for workout. The fast-paced music keeps your energy in check and makes exercise enjoyable. Exercise often needs motivation, and good music can be the perfect motivation for your workout session.

Ambient frequencies can make you creative

Loud music can be distracting for your work at times, but ambient or moderately loud music can be really great for your creative work. It helps the creative genius in you to give shape to your creation. If you play some mild music in the background while working on any kind of creative project, it can make it more enjoyable and easy-going.

Music is like fuel for artists. The soothing ambiance of the music can inspire their creations and it also helps them to stay focused while working. So, it's definitely a good idea to listen to some tunes while you work.

If you have ADHD, music makes you more creative. Sounds help people with ADHD focus their attention better. And above everything, music makes you happier while you work. Happiness should be your number one priority. It's important to feel good and happy.

Can shape your personality

Music can play a major role in shaping your personality. The kind of music you like says a lot about you. It can help others predict your personality as well. People tend to get along better with people who share the same taste in music.

So, music can literally influence the way you perceive reality. Everyone has a different version of reality and in the real world, there is no equality. There is no single standard to map that everyone is equal. There is no up or down, everyone just functions a different way. Everyone is different and unique in their own way. Your music reflects your philosophies and commitments that you carry in the world. And it becomes easier to find like-minded people through your taste in music.

Music can help you build a strong character and it can lay the path for your dreams. You just have to follow your heart and believe in the possibility of it being true. The universe has a tendency to fall in love with stubborn hearts.

Make you a better human being

Music makes you a better human being on a lot of different grounds. It makes you happy, and when you're happy, you want to make everyone around you happy. It makes you feel good, so you want to make sure that everyone should have a good time. Also, it awakens that feeling of compassion and empathy for others. You should be kind to people, whenever you are in a position to help them.

Everyone needs a little motivation, every now and then. Music can inspire you, to take all those chances you were too scared to take. Always choose to heal, always choose to forgive. And as the famous saying goes, the world breaks everyone, some of us are just stronger in the broken places.

To conclude, music can help you through the rough times in your life. There will always be times when you will feel defeated and tired, but it will always be your decision if you want to stay that way or change for the better. So, next time, turn your music loud, and dance with it until you feel good again. You can harness the power and positivity of music to stand back up on your feet.

Let it be the light at the end of the tunnel. Choose to be brave in the face of life. And choose to be happy. As long as you're happy, know that you won life. If you truly understand that one small fact, you can play death on the strings of life the way Orpheus did. Lose yourself in the music, and you will find yourself in the process.
